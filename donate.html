How You Can Help

The Camels Hump Coders are a Vermont-based FIRST LEGO League team giving middle-school students hands-on experience with robotics, coding, and problem-solving. We're fundraising to support our season and keep the team free and accessible to all students.

Every contribution makes a direct impact. All donations are tax-deductible.

We also welcome in-kind donations and sponsorships.

Thank you for helping us inspire the next generation of engineers and innovators.

Your donation helps us:

  • Cover $2,500 in annual registration & competition fees
  • Design and build our robot (parts, sensors, materials)
  • Support competition travel and lodging
  • Provide mentoring, workshops, and learning resources
