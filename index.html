Welcome to Camels Hump Coders!

2025-2026 Season: "Unearthed!"

We are a middle school robotics team from Huntington, Vermont, participating in FIRST® LEGO® League (FLL).

Our Mission

We love to build, create, and invent! Our team is passionate about STEM learning through hands-on robotics, coding, and engineering challenges.

Robotics

Building and programming LEGO robots to complete missions and solve real-world problems.

Innovation

Developing creative solutions through research and collaborative problem-solving.

Teamwork

Learning together as middle school students united by our shared interest in technology.

Robot Design

Engineering creative and efficient robot designs to tackle complex challenges and achieve mission objectives.

What is FIRST® LEGO® League?

FLL is an international youth robotics program that combines:

  • Science and technology exploration
  • Building and programming LEGO robots
  • Coding missions and challenges
  • Innovation projects addressing real-world issues
