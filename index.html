We are a middle school robotics team from Huntington, Vermont, participating in FIRST® LEGO® League (FLL).

Our Team

Hello! We're the Camels Hump Coders, a group of middle school students from Huntington, Vermont. We all love to build, create and invent. We have a team that competes in FLL, First Lego League, which is an international program for kids to develop their skills in building, engineering and coding as well as other STEM topics. This program has a huge science component along with building a lego robot and coding various missions. One of our teammates had experience with the league and wanted to form his own team. He asked his group of friends if they were interested and they agreed. We officially formed the team in 2023 and since then we have made some amazing accomplishments.

What is FIRST® LEGO® League?

From the FLL home webpage:

Designed for ages 4 to 16 (age ranges vary by region), FIRST LEGO League features three age-appropriate divisions that build upon each other—from playful discovery to real-world problem-solving through coding, engineering, and research. Students around the world come together to take on themed challenges and connect at high-energy local and global events that celebrate creativity, collaboration, and innovation.

About FLL

The components of FLL are:

Robot Game

Building and programming LEGO® robots to complete missions set up Throughout the game bord to earn points in a 2:30 round.

Innovation

Developing creative solutions through research and collaborative problem-solving to solve real world issues, and present them in a creative style.

Core Values

Respecting our teammates and competitors throughout all of the entire sesions and competition.

Robot Design

Engineering creative and efficient robot designs to tackle complex challenges and achieve mission objectives in order to earn points.
