Welcome to Camels Hump Coders!

2025-2026 Season: "Unearthed!"

We are a middle school robotics team from Huntington, Vermont, participating in FIRST® LEGO® League (FLL).

Our Mission

We love to build, create, and invent! Our team is passionate about STEAM learning through hands-on robotics, coding, engineering, and team building activities.

Robotics

Building and programming LEGO robots to complete missions set up Throughout the game bord to earn points in a 2:30 round.

Innovation

Developing creative solutions through research and collaborative problem-solving to solve real world issues.

Core Values

Respecting our teammates and competitors throughout our entire sesions and competition.

Robot Design

Engineering creative and efficient robot designs to tackle complex challenges and achieve mission objectives in order to earn points.

What is FIRST® LEGO® League?

FLL is an international youth robotics program that combines:

  • Science and technology exploration
  • Building and programming LEGO robots
  • Coding missions and challenges
  • Innovation projects addressing real-world issues
