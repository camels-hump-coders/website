Our First Season Journey

The 2023-2024 season marked the official formation of the Camels Hump Coders! This was our inaugural year as a team, bringing together middle school students from Huntington who share a passion for robotics, coding, and innovation.

üéØ Key Milestones

  • Team Formation: United by one experienced teammate who brought friends together
  • First Competition: Vermont State Qualifier - North at Norwich University
  • Learning Experience: Built our first competition robot
  • Community Engagement: Presented our project to local organizations

Robot Design & Missions

In our first season, we designed and programmed a robot to complete art-themed missions including:

  • Delivering art supplies to different zones
  • Creating collaborative art pieces
  • Setting up museum exhibits
  • Building performance stages
  • Connecting cultural centers

What We Learned

Teamwork

Working together as a new team, we learned to combine our different strengths and perspectives to solve challenges creatively.

Problem-Solving

Every robot mission taught us to think critically, debug our code, and iterate on our designs.

Communication

Presenting our innovation project helped us develop confidence in sharing our ideas with judges and the community.

Competition Experience

Competing at the Vermont State Qualifier - North at Norwich University was an incredible experience for our newly formed team. We:

  • Competed against established teams from across Vermont
  • Presented our innovation project to expert judges
  • Completed robot game missions on the official table
  • Made connections with other teams and mentors
  • Gained valuable experience that set us up for future success
