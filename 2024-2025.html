Our Innovation Project: Measuring Sea Ice Thickness

Our team focused on a critical aspect of climate change research: accurately measuring sea ice thickness. This data is essential for:

  • Understanding climate change impacts
  • Predicting future sea level changes
  • Protecting Arctic ecosystems
  • Improving navigation safety in polar regions
