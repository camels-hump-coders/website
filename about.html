About Our Team

Middle School Robotics Team from Huntington, Vermont

Meet Our Team

We are a close-knit crew of builders, coders, and problem-solvers who learn from each other every day.

Our Story

We are a group of middle school students from Huntington, Vermont who share a passion for building, creating, and inventing. Our team formed recently when one experienced teammate brought together students with a shared interest in technology and innovation.

"We all love to build, create and invent." - This simple statement captures the essence of who we are and what drives us forward.

As participants in the FIRST® LEGO® League, we engage in:

  • Science and technology exploration
  • Building and programming LEGO robots
  • Coding missions to solve challenges
  • Developing innovative solutions to real-world problems

What We Do

Robot Design

We design, build, and program autonomous robots using LEGO MINDSTORMS® technology to complete missions on a themed playing field.

Innovation Project

We research real-world problems and develop innovative solutions, presenting our findings to judges and the community.

Core Values

We embody the FIRST® Core Values of discovery, innovation, impact, inclusion, teamwork, and fun!

Join Our Journey

As we continue to grow and learn, we're excited about the challenges ahead. Whether it's debugging code, perfecting our robot's performance, or brainstorming innovative solutions, every day brings new opportunities to expand our skills and knowledge.

We're proud to represent Huntington, Vermont in the international FIRST® LEGO® League community, demonstrating that students from rural communities can excel in STEM fields and make meaningful contributions to solving global challenges.
