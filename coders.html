Getting Started

New to coding? Here's how to begin your journey:

1. Start with Basics

Begin with visual programming tools like Scratch to understand programming concepts without worrying about syntax.

2. Practice Regularly

Try the Hour of Code activities or Khan Academy lessons to build your skills step by step.

3. Apply to Robotics

Use Pybricks or the LEGO programming environment to bring your code to life with our robots.
